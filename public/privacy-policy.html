Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2024

Overview

This HTML to Markdown Converter extension is designed with your privacy in mind. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we handle data.

Data Collection

This extension only processes the text you explicitly select on web pages. We do not:

  • Collect or store any personal information
  • Track your browsing history
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Use cookies or other tracking mechanisms

How Your Data is Used

The extension only:

  • Accesses the text you explicitly select on web pages
  • Converts the selected HTML to Markdown format
  • Copies the converted Markdown to your clipboard

All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services that collect or process your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
